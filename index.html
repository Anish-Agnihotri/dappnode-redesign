Benefits of DAppNode

DAppNode simplifies the process of connecting to the decentralized internet by allowing a user to conveniently host P2P clients.


Easy setup.

Trustless system.

Invitingly open source.

Free up space on your devices.

Great for Proof of Stake mining.

You can browse .eth domains like the normal web.

You can take control of your nodes.

Encourages people to have their own server.

Perfect for projects that reward you for hosting (Filecoin, Mysterium, etc.).

Can facilitate a P2P economy.

Host any P2P networking protocols.

DApp teams can develop packages their users can run 24/7.

Never trust a hosting provider with your keys again.

One-click node deployment (full nodes, DApps).

Censorship resistant (run your own VPN).

One DAppNode can support an entire community.

DAppNode will build communities and decrease centralization.

DAppNode is the most important piece of infrastructure for our decentralized future.

Considering all of the incentive structures to run nodes, most DAppNode admins will make a profit maintining their node.
